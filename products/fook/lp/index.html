Fook - Food Expense Book

Manage your food expenses properly with Fook!

Record only food expenses

It’s a home budget book for keeping track of "food expenses" only! You can check on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis. It supports recording in all 37 types of currency.


Features

  • Food expenses of "Days", "Weeks (Last 7 Days)", "Months"
  • You can add a payment method in the notes section
  • You can freely add multiple tags in the notes section
  • We support the currencies of over 180 regions worldwide
  • There are a total of 37 types of currency
  • You can export your food expenses in CSV format
